about me

I'm pursuing a double major in computer science and mathematics (applied track) at Vanderbilt University, and will be working as a software engineer at Samsara following my graduation.

I am a teaching assistant and grader for EECE 2123, Digital Systems, and I work as an online course developer for Vanderbilt's Institute for Software Integrated Systems. I am working on a project involving the use of pupillometry in cognitive load monitoring with the MAPLE (Machine Automation, Perception, and Learning) Lab.

I'm a media/publications lead for the YX Foundation, a hybrid coalition-design lab dedicated to creating and analyzing deep tech through the framework of critical race theory.

On campus, I serve as the chair of Vanderbilt Student Government's Campus Alliance committee, a volunteer with the Vanderbilt Climate Change Initiative, and a board member for my dance team, Vandy Raas :)

My passions lie in the potential uses of technology to promote human welfare and education, with a special focus on algorithmic bias and the safe development/governence of Artificial Intelligence. My relevant technical skills include C++, Java, Python
